About

I am a medical doctor specializing in radiology. My passion is improving people’s lives by impacting their health care and tackling poverty. I do this by developing and implementing technology to help doctors obtain the care they need for their patients , thinking through the business of health care and connecting people that should be working together.

I enjoy reading books, travel , and hacking on open source software.I am an entrepreneur , running a social enterprise to tackle health care in Sub Saharan Africa.
